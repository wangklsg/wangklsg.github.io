Bio

I am currently an associate professor (with tenure) in the School of CSE at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I worked as a research fellow in the School of Computing (SoC), National University of Singapore (NUS) from 2021 to 2023. I received my PhD degree from SoC NUS. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor's degree with the first class honours from Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

I am broadly interested in Trustworthy AI, Secure and Private Software Engineering. In particular, my current research focuses on the Safety and Ethics Assessment as well as Interpretability and Explainability of Large Language Models. I have published in various top-tier conferences and journals such as OOPSLA, NDSS, MobiCom, TSE, TOSEM, ICSE, FSE, ASE, ISSTA and WWW.
